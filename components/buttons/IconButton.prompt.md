**IconButton** — circular icon-only button for social links and compact actions.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Instagram" href="#" variant="outline"><InstagramIcon/></IconButton>
<IconButton label="Email" variant="solid"><MailIcon/></IconButton>
```

Variants: `outline` (card surface + hairline, turns pink and lifts on hover), `solid` (gradient pink + glow). Sizes `sm | md | lg`. Always pass `label` for accessibility.
